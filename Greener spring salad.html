Greener Spring salad

DESCRIPTION

Properly garnished spring salad made with all of natures freshest Ingredients

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 bunch of asparagus,tender parts,chopped into 1-inch pieces
  • Three carrots
  • 5 sticks of celery
  • some fresh tomatoes
  • 2 radishes,thinly sliced
  • Handful of salad greens
  • 1/2 cup of crumbled feta chesse
  • sea salt and freshly ground pepper

Dressing

  • 1/4 cup fresh basil or a mix of basil and mint
  • 1 small garlic clove
  • 1 tablespoon of lemon juice, plus 1/2 teaspoon of zest
  • 1 tablespoon white wine vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil, more as desired
  • 1/4 teaspoon sea salt

RECIPES

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to oil and set a bowl of ice water nearby. Blanch the asperagus for about 1 minute, until tender but still bright green. Transfer to the ice water for 1 minute,then drain. Allow the asparagus to dry and transfer it back to the bowl and and add the peas.
  2. Make the dressing: In a food processor, pulse together the herbs,galic, lemon juice, zest,vinegar, olive oil and salt.
  3. Add half of the dressing to the bowl with the asperagous and toss to coat. Season to taste with salt and pepper.
  4. Assemble the salad layer by layer. After that, drizzle with dressing then add for salt and pepper to taste and serve.
